FAQ
What people ask first.
What roles can Pods cover?+
Software dev, UI/UX, motion graphics, marketing, customer support, and operations. If a startup needs it and it ships remotely, we build a Pod for it.
How do you verify work is happening?+
Every Pod member works from Blue Ox Kampus — daily attendance, guaranteed infrastructure, Pod Coordinator on-site. Weekly Pod Report every Friday.
What if someone drops mid-project?+
Replaced from our ranked database at no extra cost. Campus model means backups are on-site. 3–5 business days.
Timezone overlap for EU/US/Gulf?+
Uganda = UTC+3. Full European overlap. US East: 3–4 hours. Gulf: 1 hour ahead — nearly identical business hours.
Do talent pay anything?+
No. Startups pay the program fee. Talent get free campus, competitions, placement, and career support.
Can placements be grant-funded?+
Yes. Impact track places refugee talent via IOM, UNHCR, EU-Africa funds. Subsidised rates + ESG documentation.
